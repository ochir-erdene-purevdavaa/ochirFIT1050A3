Professional Bookkeeping

We at IKTB are bookkeeping professionals and have been making sure that our client's accounts are perfect for over 15 years. We are bookkeeping experts and have helped numerous large and small businesses solve all their account problems. Our bookkeepers are experts in XERO, MYOB, Quickbooks, and all other popular accounts and bookkeeping software.

What We Offer

IKTB Business Services can offer a wide range of services to someone who needs help managing their finances. We can provide accurate and timely financial information, help with tax preparation, and ensure that all financial records are in compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

Our Beginnings

IKTB Business Services was founded by Melissa Orchard, who started IKTB Business Services in 2007. She worked with many Small and Medium Businesses and developed trust with the local businesses in South East Melbourne. Now IKTB is one of the popular Accounting and Bookkeeping service providers and makes sure all our clients' problems are solved.
